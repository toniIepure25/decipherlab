# Pre-Submission Checklist

- Anonymous submission metadata verified in `main.tex`
- No author names in the manuscript
- No email addresses in the manuscript
- No affiliations in the manuscript
- No acknowledgements in the manuscript
- Camera-ready metadata stored separately in `camera_ready_metadata.md`
- LNCS class and bibliography files present locally under `paper/icdar_das/template`
- References compile with `splncs04`
- Main figure and main table checked in the LNCS PDF
- Appendix artifacts kept secondary
- Title and abstract finalized
- Title shortened for cleaner front-page balance
- Table 1 wording tightened for faster scanning
- Estimated page count verified under the 17-page DAS limit
- Final PDF reviewed for double-blind compliance
