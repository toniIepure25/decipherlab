# Anonymity Checklist

## Verified for the submission manuscript

1. No author names appear in the manuscript body or front matter.
2. No email addresses appear in the manuscript body or front matter.
3. No affiliation metadata appears in the manuscript body or front matter.
4. No acknowledgements section appears in the submission version.
5. No obvious self-identifying repository phrasing remains in the manuscript text.
6. Real metadata is stored separately in `camera_ready_metadata.md`.

## Wording checks performed

- Removed explicit project-repository wording from the abstract and bibliography notes.
- Removed internal workflow phrases such as `branch` and `workshop package` from the submission manuscript.
- Kept dataset names because they are part of the evidence base, not author identity.
