# Pre-Submission Checklist

- Confirm final author names, affiliations, and emails in `main.tex`.
- Replace `Affiliation TODO` with the final affiliation line.
- Decide whether any acknowledgment or funding line must be added back.
- Review `bib/references.bib` and replace local placeholder-style dataset metadata if fuller citation metadata is available.
- Verify all manuscript citations after the final bibliography pass.
- Confirm page length and appendix policy for the chosen DAS 2026 track.
- Re-run `pdflatex -> bibtex -> pdflatex -> pdflatex` after bibliography completion.
- Check that title and abstract remain free of math, footnotes, and unsupported symbols.
- Verify that figures render legibly in two-column layout.
- Verify that tables fit cleanly in IEEE conference formatting without clipping.
- Re-read limitations and conclusion to ensure real vs synthetic claims remain separated.
