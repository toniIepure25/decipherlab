# Pre-Submission Checklist

- Confirm that the single-author identity in `main.tex` matches the intended submission metadata exactly.
- Decide whether any acknowledgment or funding line must be added back.
- Verify that every bibliography entry in `bib/references.bib` matches the intended final citation style.
- Re-run `pdflatex -> bibtex -> pdflatex -> pdflatex` after the final metadata pass.
- Check that the main figure remains legible in two-column review printouts.
- Check that Table I remains easy to scan in grayscale printouts.
- Re-read the abstract, introduction, and conclusion together to confirm that the central claim wording matches exactly.
- Confirm that the appendix policy of the final DAS track allows the current support material layout.
- Verify that `Figure 1`, `Table I`, `Figure A1`, `Table A1`, and `Table A2` appear after first citation and with stable numbering.
- Confirm that the appendix break before `\appendices` still looks deliberate in the final camera-ready PDF.
- Re-read limitations and reviewer-confusion notes to ensure real vs synthetic evidence remains clearly separated.
