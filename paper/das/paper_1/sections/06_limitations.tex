\section{Limitations}

The paper's main limitation is not hidden. The strongest higher-level positive evidence is still partly synthetic-from-real, and the real downstream structural result remains mixed rather than replicated.

More specifically:

\begin{itemize}
\item real grouped top-k rescue replicates, but exact grouped recovery does not;
\item the redesigned real downstream task has much better coverage, but exact gains remain selective;
\item conformal helps in some real support regimes, not universally;
\item Historical Newspapers has a much stronger validation chain than before, but still not an independent multi-annotator gold campaign; and
\item no real downstream family or process target is available yet.
\end{itemize}

These limitations define the paper's boundary rather than undermine its contribution. The strongest value of the branch is that it explains why propagation often fails instead of simply reporting that it fails.
