# Title Options

## Recommended Title

**Preserving Transcription Uncertainty Improves Symbol-Level Glyph Reasoning Under Ambiguity: Evidence from Three Real Datasets**

Why this is recommended:

- It states the core mechanism: preserving transcription uncertainty.
- It states the scope: symbol-level reasoning under ambiguity.
- It signals the empirical contribution: three real datasets.
- It avoids semantic-decipherment overclaiming.
