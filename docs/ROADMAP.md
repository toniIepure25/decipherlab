# Roadmap

## MVP

- Synthetic image-to-hypothesis pipeline
- Top-k transcription posteriors
- Glyph clustering baseline
- Structural triage and null comparisons
- Heuristic hypothesis ranking
- Evaluation and ablation harness

## Current Paper-Credibility Step

- Split-aware real glyph-crop manifest adapter
- Calibrated posterior model with train/val/test handling
- Ambiguity sweep for four-way baseline comparison
- Symbol-level calibration and recovery metrics
- Paper-style experiment summary artifacts

## Next Paper-Grade Step

- Real historical cipher image adapters
- Stronger posterior generation and calibration
- Stronger family-specific inference engines
- Better transposition and nomenclator support
- Historical transfer experiments

## Risky Research Extensions

- Joint Bayesian transcription-decipherment search
- Scribal-hand clustering with mixed inventories
- Unknown-language alignment with phonetic or family priors
- Hybrid artifact detection for layered systems

## Future Publishable Directions

- Uncertainty propagation from manuscript image to decipherment
- Hallucination-resistant evaluation for decipherment systems
- Multi-world inference across cipher, pseudo-text, and rare-script regimes
- Synthetic-to-historical transfer benchmarks for low-resource decipherment
